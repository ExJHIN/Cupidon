Privacy policy

Wild Bear Ads Media Group respects privacy of Internet Users and does not collect personally identifying information without the Internet User’s permission.

The Wild Bear Ads Privacy Policy describes how we treat personal information when you use Wild Bear Ads’ services, including information provided when you use Wild Bear Ads. In addition, the following describes our privacy practices that are specific to Wild Bear Ads.

Collection of Data
Wild Bear Ads does not collect identity or contact information from Internet Users to this Web site. Wild Bear Ads may however, collect non-personally identifiable information, for example, browser type, operating system, time spent on the Web site, etc. This information may be used for system, Web site, and process improvements.

Wild Bear Ads does not sell or rent any personally identifying information. Wild Bear Ads does not disclose personally identifying information to anyone that is not authorized to do so.

Collection of Information by Third-Party Sites Wild Bear Ads contains links to other Internet Web sites, including those sites of credit card issuers. Wild Bear Ads has no control or authority over the information submitted or collected by these third-party Web site(s). Please refer to the respective Privacy Policy of the individual third party Web site(s).

Spam
Wild Bear Ads does not intentionally send unsolicited e-mail messages that could legitimately be construed as spam.

COPPA
Wild Bear Ads complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998. Wild Bear Ads is not specifically directed to children under 18.

More information
Wild Bear Ads adheres to the US Safe Harbor privacy principles. For more information about the Safe Harbor framework or our registration, see the Department of Commerce’s web site.

If you have additional questions, please contact us any time.
